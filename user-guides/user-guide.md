# Photographer Platform User Guide

This guide walks photographers, studio owners, admins, collaborators, and clients through the core workflows of the Photographer Platform. It covers account access, project and client management, gallery delivery, store operations, websites, billing, analytics, integrations, and support.

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Create your account
1. Open the sign-up page.
2. Enter your name, business email, and password.
3. Accept the terms and create the account.
4. Verify your email to activate the account.

## 16. Troubleshooting

### 16.1 Can’t sign in
- Verify your email and password.
- Use “Forgot password” to reset credentials.
- Check email verification status.

### 16.2 Upload issues
- Confirm file size and format.
- Try a smaller batch.
- Check your storage limits.

### 16.3 Payments failing
- Confirm payment method details.
- Check billing status and plan limits.

### 16.4 Gallery access problems
- Verify the access level (public, unlisted, password-protected).
- Confirm download permissions.

## 17. Glossary

- **Gallery**: A curated set of photos delivered to clients.
- **Project**: A top-level record that groups clients, galleries, and orders.
- **Proofing**: Client review process using favorites and comments.
- **Store**: The e-commerce area for prints and digital products.
- **Template**: A website design used in the portfolio builder.
